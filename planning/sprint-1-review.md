## 3. Stakeholder Feedback

### 3.1 Positive Feedback

- Basic booking flow works smoothly
- Cancellation feature is user-friendly and intuitive
- Calendar interface clean and professional

### 3.2 Concerns & Questions

- Dependencies between stories were not clearly communicated during planning
- How does double-booking prevention work with multiple concurrent users?
- Clarification needed on maximum booking duration limits

### 3.3 Adjustment Requests

- Replace vague terminology in Story #2 ("set a pattern") with specific language ("schedule recurring meetings")
- Add visual indicators for room availability status on calendar
- Include capacity information in room selection view
